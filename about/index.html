DYLAN WESTLAND is a designer who implements intersection between aesthetics and utility.

I work across all medians of design, my skills intersect between digital and physical design.

My goal as both an artist and an engineer is to explore all aspects of design and, through the overlaps I encounter, innovate and change the current status quo of what defines 'design'.
